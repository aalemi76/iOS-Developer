*** Wellcome to Bull's Eye ***

Bull's Eye is a simple but fascinating game where you can win points and fame by dragging a slider.

Your goal is to place the slider as close as possible to the target value and then push the "Hit me!" button. The closer you are, the more points you score. Finally, if you want to start it over just hit the "Start Over?" button. Remind that by doing so, your scores will reset to 0.

Have a good time!
